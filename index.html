Our Mission

At The Bolah Girl Initiative, we aim to raise awareness about climate change and its effects during extreme weather events such as droughts and floods. We emphasize how these environmental challenges disproportionately affect households and often place an extra burden on women, who frequently take on more work to secure family livelihoods.

During extreme weather such as droughts and floods, women tend to work more to secure household livelihoods.

With a sharp focus on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), The Bolah Girl Initiative positions itself as both an environmental watchdog and a champion of female empowerment. We are committed to creating sustainable solutions that address both climate challenges and gender inequality.

Our Programs

#10kTrees4Kaduna Campaign

The #10kTrees4Kaduna campaign is our flagship tree planting initiative aimed at combating desertification and improving local ecosystems in Kaduna State. Working with local communities, we plant indigenous trees that help restore biodiversity and provide economic benefits.

  • Community-led tree planting events
  • Education on tree care and maintenance
  • Creation of sustainable tree nurseries
  • Monitoring and evaluation of tree survival rates

In partnership with @bfpinitiative and @acikaduna, we've already planted thousands of trees across multiple communities in Northern Nigeria.

Join Our Next Planting Event

Climate Change Education

Our climate education program aims to build knowledge and awareness about climate change impacts and solutions among communities in Northern Nigeria. Through workshops, school programs, and public events, we empower people with the knowledge they need to adapt to climate challenges.

  • School-based climate literacy programs
  • Community workshops on climate adaptation
  • Training for local climate champions
  • Development of locally relevant educational materials

By making climate science accessible and relevant to local contexts, we help communities understand and respond to the changes they're experiencing.

Request a Workshop

Women's Climate Leadership

Our Women's Climate Leadership program recognizes that women are often on the frontlines of climate change impacts yet underrepresented in decision-making. We provide training, resources, and support for women to become climate leaders in their communities.

  • Leadership and advocacy skills development
  • Training in sustainable livelihoods
  • Support for women-led climate initiatives
  • Creation of women's support networks

By empowering women as climate leaders, we strengthen community resilience while addressing gender inequality.

Join the Program

Building Resilient Communities

Our community resilience program helps vulnerable communities prepare for and adapt to climate impacts through locally-led initiatives. We work with communities to identify their specific climate challenges and develop sustainable solutions.

  • Community-based vulnerability assessments
  • Development of local adaptation plans
  • Implementation of climate-smart agriculture
  • Water conservation and management projects

By building on local knowledge and strengthening existing community structures, we help create lasting resilience to climate challenges.
